Popular Knowledge Base Articles

How to Upgrade My Subscription

To upgrade, navigate to the Pricing plans. You can select Premium, Professional, or Enterprise tiers, choose Monthly or Annual cycles, and apply promotional coupon codes like SAVE10 or WELCOME50 for instant discounts.

Harnessing the AI Assistant

Use the AI chatbot or spoken voice controls to ask "Where did I spend most?" or "How much did I spend on food?". The AI advisor parses dates, aggregate categories, predicts next month's spending, and designs 1, 5, or 10-year compound savings projections.

Securing Your Account with MFA

Navigate to the Settings page and select "Enable Multi-Factor Authentication (MFA via OTP)". Upon your next sign-in, you will receive a secure 6-digit verification code printed to your server/email log to complete entry.

GDPR Data Portability & Rights

You hold full control over your private information. Under the GDPR controls in Settings, you can trigger a full export of your personal database items as a clean JSON layout or purge your account permanently from the server.

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I change my primary currency format?
Go to the Settings page, select your currency choice (INR, USD, EUR, GBP, JPY, AED, or SGD) from the dropdown list, and click Save Preferences. All your dashboards, charts, and budget progress charts will update automatically to reflect this currency.
Can I restore deleted transaction logs or accounts?
No. In compliance with GDPR and strict consumer privacy guidelines, when you choose to Purge Account or delete specific transactions, the database rows are instantly scrubbed permanently. We do not store backups of your deleted entries. Make sure to download a JSON export before purging if you need to keep records.
What voice languages does the tracker support?
SmartSpend voice command engine currently supports English, Spanish, French, and Hindi. You can switch your preferred voice input language from the dropdown menu in Settings, where you can also customize the Text-To-Speech (TTS) audio narration speeds.
